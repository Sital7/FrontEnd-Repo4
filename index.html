Practice Areas

Bankruptcy Law

Bankruptcy law provides legal solutions for individuals or businesses to manage or eliminate debt while offering protection from creditors.

Family Law

Family law addresses legal matters related to family relationships, including marriage, divorce, child custody, and adoption.

Criminal Defense

Criminal defense involves legal representation and advocacy for individuals accused of crimes to protect their rights and ensure a fair trial.

Corporate Law

Corporate law governs the formation, operation, and compliance of businesses, addressing legal matters like contracts, mergers, and governance.

Business Law

Business law covers legal aspects related to starting, operating, and managing a business, including contracts, disputes, and regulatory compliance.

Immigration Law

Immigration law deals with the legal processes and regulations governing the entry, stay, and rights of individuals in a country.
